# 🚀 NextPath AI

NextPath AI is an AI-powered career guidance platform that helps students and professionals plan, build, and accelerate their careers using the power of Google Gemini AI.

### 🌐 [Live Demo Link](https://db7f43cd9f8bb5.lhr.life/)

---

## 🌟 Overview
NextPath AI is a full-stack AI Career Coach developed as part of the **Google Build With AI Bootcamp – Bhilai**. It provides personalized career guidance, resume evaluation, interview preparation, learning plans, and AI-powered career assistance through a sleek, modern dashboard.

![NextPath AI Dashboard](Screenshot%202026-06-27%20220342.png)

---

## ✨ Features

*   **📄 AI Resume Analysis:** Evaluate your resume score and get actionable feedback.
*   **🛣️ Personalized Career Roadmap:** Tailored milestones based on your professional goals.
*   **💼 AI Cover Letter Generator:** Draft compelling cover letters instantly.
*   **💡 LinkedIn Headline Optimizer:** Boost your profile's searchability and professional impact.
*   **🎯 Skill Gap Analysis:** Identify core gaps between your current skill set and industry expectations.
*   **📅 Daily Learning Planner:** Curate focused learning schedules with manageable daily tasks.
*   **🎤 AI Mock Interview:** Practice real-world technical and soft-skill interview loops.
*   **💻 Coding Practice:** Solve algorithmic challenges directly in an integrated web editor.
*   **💰 Salary Insights:** Get deep analytics on income brackets categorized by region and job title.
*   **🤖 Gemini AI Career Chat Assistant:** Chat 24/7 with a dedicated context-aware virtual coach.

*   **Backend:** Node.js, Express.js
*   **AI Engine:** Google Gemini API
*   **Deployment & Infrastructure:** Docker, Render, Vercel
